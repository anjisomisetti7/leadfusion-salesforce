# 🚀 LeadFusion App Link :- https://ai-powered-lead-generation-system-1048640657551.asia-southeast1.run.app

> **Capture. Qualify. Convert.**

LeadFusion AI is an AI-powered Lead Generation and CRM Automation platform built to help businesses identify, qualify, and convert potential customers efficiently. It combines Artificial Intelligence with Salesforce automation to streamline the complete lead management process, from capturing customer information to generating actionable sales insights.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

LeadFusion AI captures leads through intelligent forms and an AI chatbot, analyzes customer data using AI, assigns lead scores, predicts conversion probability, and automatically pushes qualified leads into Salesforce CRM. The platform also automates follow-up tasks, notifications, and reporting to improve sales productivity.
